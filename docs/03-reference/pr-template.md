# Reference: Pull Request Template

A pull request is a review package when an artifact may land or the branch materially helps a decision. It is not the lifecycle.

The starter is [`templates/.github/PULL_REQUEST_TEMPLATE.md`](../../templates/.github/PULL_REQUEST_TEMPLATE.md).

## What the PR should make easy to judge

- the outcome or decision;
- the proposed resolution and whether anything landed or released;
- the criteria, gates, and evidence;
- scope, risk, rollout, rollback, and continuing obligations;
- resource actuals and material variance when measured;
- the decision evidence when nothing should land;
- the learning or initiative update.

A merged PR may still be unreleased. A closed PR may support a strong decision not to continue. A closed PR with weak evidence may remain unresolved loss.

Do not make a successful path look artificially cheap by omitting failed or discarded attempts.
